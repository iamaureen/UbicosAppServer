Buggy Lab: Collect Your Data

Watch the Tumble Buggy video. Observe the moving object. What do you notice? Do you think, speed is a proportional relationship?

Think about the following:

  • What measurements should we take?
  • What units will we use?
  • Do we plan to collect more than one set of data?
  • What procedures do we need to follow so that our data is as accurate and comparable as possible?
