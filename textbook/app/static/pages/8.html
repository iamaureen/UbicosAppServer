Help Cobi

Part of this project is having you teach a computer “agent,” or program. When you are teaching someone else, it is often helpful to know the process and the answers before you begin. Solve the problems in your workbook Pg. 5 through Pg. 8 in preparation for helping this computerized student Cobi.

If you need help with ratios, watch the Khan Academy video on Finding Ratios: An Introduction.
