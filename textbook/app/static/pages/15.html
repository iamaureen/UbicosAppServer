Continue the Conversation

Now that it has been a few days since you made your post on Khan Academy, it's time to go back and help others again! Check the Proportional Relationship link and scroll to the comment section. (Remember, you don't need to watch the video again).

Find other students' post (Use Sort By: Recent) and see if you can reply back to them. Think about the talk moves to help you think of an appropriate response:

  • Do you disagree with their response and want to ask for their reasoning?
  • Are they understanding the content and you want to give a complement?
  • Do you want to go further with the idea and brainstorm?

Click on the Lightbulb Icon (Khan Academy tool) to write your response in the Modelbook (not on Khan Academy.) Use the help button for hints on how to earn a badge. Select answer a question. Take a screenshot of your discussion so far. Then write your response. The Modelbook tool will post on Khan Academy for you.
